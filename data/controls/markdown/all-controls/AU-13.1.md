## Control Statement
Monitor open-source information and information sites using automated mechanisms for monitoring open-source information and information sites are defined;.

## Implementation Guidance
Automated mechanisms include commercial services that provide notifications and alerts to organizations and automated scripts to monitor new posts on websites.
